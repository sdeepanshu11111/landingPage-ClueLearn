Why does ClueLearn even exist?

Because it's about our kids. Because it about future of our kids.

Let's elaborate on this.

Every Kid is different. But who knows this best. Who else. They are Us. They are Parents. As parents we do everything for our kids. We try and do our best for our kids present and their future.

But what exactly we do when it comes to make them a Better Learner. How can we make them more effective learner. How can we prepare them to help them learn anything when they grow up. As Parents we have very important role to play to make them a Natural Learner for life.

So to answer that question Why we Exist?

We exist to

empower parents to make their kids a better and a natural learner for life.

We exist to

empower parents to know about their kids exact knowledge level directly and without involvement of any one else.

We exist to

make parents - kids interactions fun-filled and learning driven.

We exist to

empower parents to achieve all of these very efficiently and effectively.

So how exactly does ClueLearn empower parents?

ClueLearn is first of its kind Q&A based Parents - Kids Engagement App for Casual Yet Effective Learning!

Parents Can Select & Assign Specially Curated Questions from Range of Topics for Kids of All Ages!

Parents Can Also Create Their Own Image & Text based Questions for Practical & Fun Learning!

Parents Can Maximize Engagement and Learning Outcomes Through Thoughtfully Designed Interfaces & Answer Flow!

Parents Can Assign, Evaluate, Reassign and Track Kids Learning Anytime & Anywhere!
